"GUARDIAN OF THE DEEP - PROTECTING A YOUNG ORPHAN"
(Approx. 21-1/2" height x 15-1/2" width x 9" depth)

Mermaid guiding a young orphaned dolphin back to his family. This sculpture is depicted in the original fired clay and two cold cast bronze patinas and is ready for reproduction. The casting will be limited to 100 numbered pieces which will be offered in either bronze or "cold cast" bronze (filled polyurethane with a thin coating of bronze). Each piece will be cast upon receipt of one-half of the purchase price with the balance due prior to shipment. A numbered certificate of authenticity signed by the artist will accompany each sculpture.
