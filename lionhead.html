"PRIDE OF A LION"
(Approx. 17" length x 14-1/2" width)

Lion Head Wall Hanging: This bas relief wall sculpture is the perfect complement for a Safari motif and is truly unique. It is depicted as a "cold cast bronze" (filled polyurethane with a thin coating of bronze) and is also available in full bronze. Each piece will be cast upon receipt of one-half of the purchase price with the balance due prior to shipment. The sculpture is angle drilled to facilitate hanging and will also be available in a shadow box frame with choices of fabric background. Prices furnished for this mounting upon request.
